About CodeVA

CodeVA is a non-profit dedicated to bringing access to Computer Science to every Virginia student. We teach kids to code - by teaching teacher to bring Computer Science literacy to their classrooms. We partner with national partners like Code.org, Exploring Computer Science, Bootstrap World, and Project GUTS to provide quality computer science training, curriculum and support to school districts and teachers.

For districts partnering with CodeVA, we recommend you carefully read our Privacy Policy, as well as the Code.org privacy policy below before accessing or using the Code.org Code Studio Website.

CodeVA Privacy Principles

We are commited, along with code.org, to these Privacy Principles:

  1. We are committed to creating a safe and secure learning environment for our students and teachers. We take the protection of this information seriously.
  2. We collect only data that cannot be used to identify individual students. Our purpose is only to better our mission of providing access to computer science education for every Virginia student.
  3. We do not sell Personal Information or exploit it for financial gain; we do not sell ads. We are a charitable 501c3 nonprofit and our revenue comes from donations and grants. We established ourselves as a nonprofit so our mission and your trust will not be in conflict with a for-profit motive.
  4. Any student academic data provided by school district partners to 3rd party evaluators for the purpose of evaluating our courses in meeting our mission will be anonymous / de-identified (per standard industry practice).
  5. We take the protection of your information very seriously.
